Brownies

Description

Looking for the best brownie recipe on the internet? You've come to the right place! These rich, fudgy brownies are topped with a decadent homemade frosting.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • White sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Cocoa powder
  • All purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Bakign powder

Steps

  1. Melt the butter on the stove, then remove from heat and add the remaining wet ingredients.
  2. Beat in the dry ingredients.
  3. Spread the batter in the pan and bake
  4. Cut brownies when done
