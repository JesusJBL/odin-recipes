Cannelloni

Description

Imagine coming home to this Spinach and Ricotta Cannelloni...a juicy spinach and ricotta filling inside cannelloni pasta tubes, topped with a homemade tomato basil sauce and melted cheese

Ingredients

  • 400 g spinach
  • extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 x 400 g tins of quality plum tomatoes
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 a bunch of fresh basil
  • 1/2 of a lemon
  • one large egg
  • Parmesan cheese
  • 250 g ricotta
  • 150 g cannelloni
  • 2 mozzarella balls
